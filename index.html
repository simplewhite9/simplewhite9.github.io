About Me

I am a master's student in the MLV (Machine Learning & Vision) Lab at Korea University, South Korea, advised by Prof. Hyunwoo J. Kim. I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Korea University. My research interests lie in multi-modal understanding, especially in video understanding with foundation models.
